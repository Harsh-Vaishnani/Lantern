Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of graphic design services do you offer?

▼

We provide a range of various creative services from web to ads & social to print. It depends on what your requirements and brief is. We usually do a consultation before onboarding you so we’re clear with our scope of work and deliverables.

Do you work with clients in my industry?

▼

We have an extensive portfolio of clients from about 10+ different industries. We have worked with all major industries like Hospitality, Health, Personal care, and more. We can connect and discuss this in detail and you can check our portfolio of creative assets made to judge for yourself.

How long will it take to deliver the graphic design services project?

▼

It depends on the type of creatives we’re making as well as how many rounds of feedback it goes through. For example, a web banner might be easy to create and can be delivered in a week or less but creating a branding identity would be a larger job that could span a month for finalizing. As a professional creative agency, we try to maximize the output in minimum time but we’re wizards of creativity, not time.

What is your pricing structure?

▼

Usually creative services are priced either project-based or retainer-based. For services like brand identity or presentation design, clients usually opt for a project-based quote as they’re one-off services, but for social or ad creative design or email design, a retainer pricing model is better since you’re going to need a certain number of assets on monthly basis for your campaigns.

Do you offer a free consultation?

▼

Give us a call and find out! 😉

How will I be involved in the design process?

▼

We believe strongly in collaborative work and we will involve you at each stage like understanding the brief from you, asking you to share references, sitting with us to brainstorm ideas, and of course for feedback before finalizing the design. If you’re a creative soul yourself and want to be involved a bit more then go ahead!

What if I need revisions?

▼

Revision is an unavoidable part of creative services. People think and visualize differently and this difference is going to lead to revisions. Our creative process includes a few rounds of revisions so that you’re completely satisfied with what you’re getting.

Do you offer copyright transfer of the final design?

▼

Yes, you’ll get the open files of the creatives as required so you can repurpose the creatives years after if you wish.
