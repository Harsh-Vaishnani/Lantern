Frequently Asked Questions

What is SEO and why is it important for my business?

▼

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It’s the process of boosting your website’s visibility in search engine results, like Google. Think of it as getting a prime spot for an ad on TV, where you’re visible to maximum potential customers online. This results in more clicks = more leads = more business growth.

How can SEO help me achieve my business goals?

▼

SEO is your rocket fuel to online success. It drives more qualified leads, higher sales, and stronger brand awareness, translating directly into achieving your business goals, whether it’s dominating your market or attracting new customers.

What are the different types of SEO services?

▼

Commonly divided into on-page & off-page SEO services, Lantern Digital offers a full spectrum of SEO services like keyword research, on-page optimization, link building & technical SEO audits. We tailor a plan that fits your unique needs and goals.

What are the benefits of hiring an SEO agency?

▼

Our SEO experts are trained professionals who keep on top of the industry trends and updates to introduce & fine-tune the SEO strategies applied to your brand. This safeguards you from the fluctuations of the dynamic yet fast-changing nature of SEO marketing.

How long does it take to see results from SEO?

▼

SEO is a marathon, not a sprint. Tangible business results usually take 3-6 months, but you’ll see progress along the way in your website traffic and SERP ranking. Once your set business goal is achieved, it is also important to keep up with the SEO maintenance for enduring growth.

What is the difference between on-page and off-page SEO?

▼

On-page SEO optimizes your website content and technical structure. Off-page SEO builds your website’s authority through backlinks and online presence. Think of it as building a strong foundation and spreading the word – both crucial for online success.

How can I track the success of my SEO campaign?

▼

We’ll provide you with clear, easy-to-understand reports on key metrics like keyword rankings, website traffic, and leads/conversions. You’ll see the impact of our efforts firsthand.

How many keywords should I target?

▼

Quality over quantity! We’ll identify the most relevant searches for your brand and further prioritize from the list, ensuring you attract the highest traffic of maximum relevance.

How can I optimize my website content for search engines?

▼

Lantern Digital helps you create compelling content that both users and search engines love. We’ll guide you on keyword placement, structure, and more.

How do I use images and videos effectively for SEO?

▼

High-quality visuals boost engagement and improve search engine visibility. Our one-page SEO optimization plan for you will have a component to optimize images & videos for maximum impact.

What is mobile-friendly design and why is it important for SEO?

▼

Since more than 60% of the searches happen on mobile these days, a website that works well on mobile is important not only for the user but also for the search engine. Let us optimize your site for all devices so you’re delivering the best user experience.

How can I optimize my product pages for search engines?

▼

Compelling product descriptions, relevant keywords, and high-quality visuals are key. We’ll help you turn product pages into lead magnets.
